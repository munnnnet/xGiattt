Frequently Asked Questions

Which package should I choose?

We guarantee that you’ll get a great design at any pricing. The higher-priced gives more options and better support.

When selecting the package, think about how much time you’ll have and what experience you have writing briefs and working with designers. Contact us for a consultation.

What will I get?

You will get perfect for use on the web and in print and ready to use & editable. Especially, likes production-ready Adobe Illustrator file, Adobe Photoshop file, PDF file or EPS file, etc.

Can I use my own digital asset?

Yes. If have any specific images (like a logo or product photos), you need to submit them to our team with brief.

Our creator may also use stock images (purchased from a third party). If the creator use it, they’ll tell you which images they are and use watermarked versions of them in their entries.

Who owns what copyright and when?

Our creators agree to upload original artwork as package you choose.

The creator agrees to transfer the rights, and you become the owner of the copyright of creation.

Can I work with the creator once contract duration ends?

Absolutely (and we love it when that happens)! When your content has finished, you can continue the contract with the same creator or someone else.

Is there really a 100% 'money back guarantee?'

No joke. If you’re not happy with our creation, we’ll give 100% of your money back. Feel free to contact our staff about it.

We offer a 100% money-back guarantee within 60 days of payment. You can also call us anytime to help you rewrite your brief, reopen your project to ensure you get a good result and help you pick a right design. Remember, if we refund your payment, you aren’t legally entitled to use any of the content.

How can I protect my creation with an NDA?

This will automatically attach a standard NDA template to your projects. If prefer, you can also upload your own NDA.

What do I need to prepare the projects

Lots of ideas! If you’re looking for inspiration, explore Dribbble for fantastic by talented design community (for design).

Bring as many specifics as you can, too. Include the details (e.g. colors, fonts), preferred type of, style suggestions, content you like, information about your business, product photos and any other specifications that would affect.

The more you give, the better they can a creation for you!

What if I have need additional sizes/types

If you need a second creation for different business or product, or if you need multiple versions of your collateral, you have an option: you can run a second project to create the additional assets.

What there really about unlimited revision?

Not Joke! We provide unlimited revision as you want. To protect our creators, we allow you to request revisioan that relate with the project. If you change the concept or have new idea about, you need make new project.
